🤝 Outreach Agent

AI affiliate recruiter — find creators, write personalized pitches, work the pipeline

How this stays safe: This tool helps you write personalized affiliate pitches fast, but you send them — through a creator's public business-inquiry email (the one in their channel "About" page). It does not auto-DM or scrape, because that breaks YouTube/Instagram/TikTok rules and gets accounts banned. Personalize, respect "no", and you'll get real replies.

⚙️ Setup

Get one at console.anthropic.com. It's saved to this device only and used to generate your messages.

Create one free in Google Cloud Console: enable "YouTube Data API v3", then make an API key. Lets the Find tab pull channels by niche.

🔎 Find creators on YouTube

Pulls channels in your niche and keeps only those in your follower range. YouTube rounds sub counts and hides some — those are skipped. Emails aren't in the API; use each result's "About page" link to grab the business email.

➕ Add a creator manually

Tip: your sweet spot is 10k–80k followers — big enough to drive sales, small enough to actually answer their email.
