# Story 4.1: Send-status in app

## Status
Draft

## Story
**As a** shop owner,
**I want** to see sent / failed / opted-out status per appointment in the app,
**so that** I know what went out and can follow up where needed.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. Each appointment shows whether a day-before and/or late reminder was sent,
   with timestamp.
2. Failed sends are clearly flagged.
3. Opted-out customers are visibly marked so the owner knows why no text went
   out.
4. The view reads from the synced `message_log` and works offline with last
   known data.

## Tasks / Subtasks
- [ ] Surface `message_log` status on the appointment row/detail (AC: 1, 2)
- [ ] Mark opted-out customers (AC: 3)
- [ ] Use synced/cached data so it renders offline (AC: 4)

## Dev Notes
Builds on the sync from Story 1.2. Keep it lightweight — a status chip per
reminder type is enough. Depends on Epics 1–3.

## Testing
- Appointment with a `sent` log → shows sent + time.
- Appointment with a `failed` log → shows failed.
- Opted-out customer → shows opted-out marker, no send.
