# Story 2.2: A2P 10DLC registration

## Status
Draft

## Story
**As a** shop owner,
**I want** my brand and campaign registered for A2P 10DLC,
**so that** US carriers deliver my business texts legally and reliably.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. A sole-proprietor **brand** is registered with legal name, address, and EIN or
   SSN.
2. A **campaign** for appointment reminders is registered and approved.
3. The sending number is attached to the approved campaign.
4. Expected one-time (~$20) and monthly fees are acknowledged by the owner.

## Tasks / Subtasks
- [ ] Gather business info: legal name, address, EIN/SSN (AC: 1)
- [ ] Submit brand registration (AC: 1)
- [ ] Submit reminders campaign with sample messages + opt-in/opt-out language
      (AC: 2)
- [ ] Attach number to campaign once approved (AC: 3)
- [ ] Confirm budget with owner (AC: 4)

## Dev Notes
**Approval currently takes ~10–15 days** (per spec) — start this early; it
blocks live sending. Sample messages must include sender identity and `STOP`
instructions. Depends on Story 2.1.

## Testing
- Verify campaign status = approved in Twilio before any live send.
